STUDIO

Welcome to the Michael Chekhov Acting Studio, home to trainings and workshops based in the work of Michael Chekhov and Clowning for Actors since 1996, in New York City.

Lenard Petit, Artistic Director, has developed the work of Michael Chekhov in a practical and imaginative way, clearly translating the technique into something easy to use for actors today. His approach is experienced in his teaching and expressed in his writing. The Michael Chekhov Handbook, for the Actor can be purchased here. Additionally a few articles by Lenard are included here for you–gratis!
